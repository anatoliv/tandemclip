Apple Silicon · macOS 13+ · Developer ID signed & notarized
🔒

LAN-only, encrypted

Every connection is PSK-TLS, keyed by a pairing code you set. No relay, no internet, no accounts.

🔁

Mirror or Manual

Auto-sync everything, or pull a specific Mac's clipboard on demand from the menu bar.

🛡️

Password-manager safe

Content marked secret by 1Password & friends is never synced. Per-Mac send/receive roles too.

⚡

Instant & self-healing

Copies propagate in well under a second, and sync survives sleep, wake, and Wi-Fi roams.
